About Us

Our services cover all the legal needs of businesses, such as company registration, government registrations & filings, accounting, documentation and annual compliance’s. Our mission is to provide one-click access to individuals and businesses for all their legal & professional needs. We are a team of Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries and Lawyers. We have our office in Delhi, Noida and Gurugram. We are the only legal firm that provides free company registration services to all the Indian and Foreign Startups.

We have more than 14 years of experience and incorporated more than 500 companies in India. We have helped more than 100 startups with legal documentation like shareholders agreements, preparing minutes of the meeting and rights issue.

We assist 1200+ companies with their regular monthly compliances like book-keeping, GST returns, TDS returns, Tax filing and secretarial compliances.
